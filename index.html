Featured · Deep Learning · 2025

Pneumonia Detection System

"AI-powered diagnostics that achieve 96% accuracy on real medical images."

The Problem

Manual screening of chest X-rays is time-consuming and prone to human error. Early detection of pneumonia is critical for patient outcomes, especially in resource-limited healthcare settings.

The Solution

An automated Computer-Aided Diagnosis (CAD) system using a fine-tuned ResNet101V2 architecture with custom hierarchical classification layers. The model leverages transfer learning, Layer Normalization, Swish activation, and Dropout regularization for robust performance.

Industry Management System

"Digitally transforming complex organizational workflows through intelligent database design."

The Problem

Organizations struggle with fragmented data across departments — employees, projects, customers, and product flows tracked in disconnected systems leading to inefficiency and data integrity issues.

The Solution

A centralized relational database architecture using MySQL with proper Entity-Relationship modeling, 3NF normalization, and referential integrity constraints. Automates complex organizational tracking across multiple departments.
